Jonathan Fulk

Coder | Francophile | Teacher


Hello. I'm Jon. I've been a French instructor at the University of Minnesota for the last 10 years, but this site is all about my other passion: web development. When I was still an undergrad in the 90s, I started creating simple websites before CSS was a thing. Many years later, I realized that, while I was busy reading Zola and learning about the latest research in Second Language Acquisition, the web dev world had left me in the dust, changing dramatically. So, by taking a series of courses, reading lots of documentation, and being mentored by a good friend and experienced software devloper, I updated my Javascript and Python skills, which renewed my passion for creating software that solves a problem and makes life better. Take a look at a few things I created below and contact me if you have a job that you would like for me to do. I'm available to freelance when not teaching, and since I'm an open-minded academic, I would happily consider any full-time opportunities you may have as well.
